Galtier heaps praise on 'great professional' Neymar after lightning start to season

Paris Saint-Germain manager Christophe Galtier has hailed Neymar as one of the world's best following his electric recent form.
I have always considered him to be one of the best players on the planet, but I have a much more informed and sharpened eye on his daily investment. Before, during and after training sessions
Galtier told reporters: He is very invested on a personal level but also in the interest of the team. He is a great professional and a great player. I discover him with pleasure every day
